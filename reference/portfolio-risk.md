# Portfolio & Risk Commands

## portfolio

Full portfolio summary: balance, positions, PnL, and exposure.

**Aliases:** `balance`, `account`, `port`

## dashboard

System dashboard with risk metrics, largest position, and funding.

**Aliases:** `dash`

## risk report

Position-level risk assessment: liquidation distances, leverage, and risk scores.

**Aliases:** `risk`

## exposure

Exposure breakdown by asset and direction.

**Aliases:** `portfolio exposure`

## rebalance

Rebalance analysis with suggestions for position sizing.

**Aliases:** `portfolio rebalance`

## capital

Portfolio capital state (hidden from help).

**Aliases:** `portfolio state`
